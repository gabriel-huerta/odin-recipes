Black Bean Soup

Description

This meatless, taco-flavored soup is loaded with bean protein and robust flavor from the combination of spices and fire-roasted tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 medium carrots, choppped
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium head. Add onion, bell peppers, celery, carrots, jalapeno peppers, and garlic; cook until veggies soften, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add diced tomateos; stir well and cook for 3 minutes. Add vegetable broth, black beans, corn, and simmer 30 minutes
