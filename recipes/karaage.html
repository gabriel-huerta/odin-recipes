Karaage (Japanese Fried Chicken)

Description

I’ve spent years sampling the best of kaarage , and finally cracked the code to make the ultimate version at home. It’s a meal my entire family begs for—including my five year old who dunks each piece in Kewpie Mayo until it’s covered! A great way to feed your loved ones and hear no complaints. EVER.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh ginger
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/4 cup Japanese soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 cup + 3 tbsp potato starch, dividedd

Steps

  1. Mix ginger, garlic, soy sauce, sake, sesame oil and ground black pepper in bowl.
  2. Cut up chicken thighs into bite sized pieces and mix into the marinade
  3. When you are ready to fry, add the eg white and 3 tablespoons of potato starch
  4. Use a heavy-bottom pot and heat 2-3 inches of oil. Fry them and enjoy!
