Lasagna

Description

A mashup between Giuliano Bugialli and Elodia Rigante's Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
  • 28-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes

Steps

  1. For the sauce, head 1/2 cup oil in large heavy Dutch oven
  2. Combine the sirloin, cheese and eggs in a large bowl
  3. Heat the remaining oil in a large skillet
